Maspik (Hebrew for 'enough') is a simple Chrome extension to help you decide whether you wish to support Israeli businesses in the light of the ongoing occupation of Palestine.

At the moment our coverage is very limited, though we hope to expand this. Please do not rely on us to ensure that you don't unwittingly aid the occupation and share ways we could improve.

Our initial motivation was the realisation that articles on the situation in Israel/Palestine would invariably be followed by some links generating revenue for one side in that conflict.

Whatever your view on that situation we found it distasteful that people were unwittingly favouring one side or the other everytime they clicked one of those links.

Maspik puts the power to decide what is right or wrong back in your hands.

What We Highlight:
